Our Goal

We want to make software that can assist people in learning math, as efficiently as possible. We want to utilize new advancements in machine learning to enable students to understand exceedingly complex subjects in easy to understand lessons.

Our Means

Some of the means of reaching our goal include creating a large database of math problems, that then train an ai to create unique math problems, and the ability to teach people how to solve them.

Our Future

In the future we plan to utilize machine learning to greatly increase the ease of learning math.
