Our Goal

The ultimate goal of todarith is to make software able to assist people in learning math, as efficiently as possible. We desire to make the site free in order to give access to the greatest amount of people possible.

Our Means

Some of the means of reaching our goal include creating a large data- base of math problems, that then train an ai to create unique math problems, and the ability to teach people how to solve them.

Our Future

In the future we plan to make instruc- tional content generated by ai to teach in the best way possible.
